Our History

Founded in 1989, Water on Wheels is a publicly listed company with extensive experience. Their expertise spans water treatment, distribution, wastewater management, and sanitation services.

Community Impact

Through their flagship program, Water on Wheels, nearly two million people from marginalized communities gained access to affordable water and good quality of services.

Vision and Commitment

Water on Wheeel's vision is to create better lives and resilient economies through critical infrastructure.

Future Outlook

Water on Wheeels continues to evolve, embracing digital technologies and smart solutions. Their vision extends beyond water provision to creating resilient, sustainable cities.
